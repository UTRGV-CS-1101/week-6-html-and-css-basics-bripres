About the Show

FRIENDS follows a group of six close-knit friends living in New York City. This fanpage highlights the main characters, favorite quotes, and a small gallery inspired by the show's vibrant style.

Main Characters

Ross Geller

Paleontology professor with a complicated love life.

Rachel Green

Fashion-savvy and the group's on-again, off-again romantic interest.

Monica Geller

Chef and the group's organized, competitive heart.

Chandler Bing

Witty and sarcastic, the comic relief with a big heart.

Joey Tribbiani

Actor and food-lover famous for "How you doin'?"

Phoebe Buffay

Quirky musician with a unique worldview.

Memorable Quotes
